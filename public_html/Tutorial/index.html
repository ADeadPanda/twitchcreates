ADeadPanda's Twitch shout out generator

Tutorial
Step 1) Click the "Generate a Twitch Acess Token" button.
Step 2) Click "Custom Scope Token"
Step 3) Scroll down and find "chat:read" and "moderation:read" make sure both are on yes!
Step 4) Scroll all the way down and click "Generate Token!".
Step 5) Click the "Authorize" button.
Step 6) Complete the Captcha
Step 7) Copy the "Acess token"
Step 8) Go back to TwitchCreates.com
Step 9) Put your twitch username in the first textbox
Step 10) paste your Access token in the 2nd textbox
Step 11) click the "Generate Overlay Link" button and copy the link at the bottom.
Step 12) Open OBS and create a new Browser source and paste the url you copied into the "url" field.
Step 13) In your Twitch chat type "!So Twitch" to test that everything is working!
**** Make sure you do not include the @ symbol in the command or it will not work! ****
